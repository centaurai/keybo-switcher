KEYBOSWITCHER

Did you write a large text in Arabic and then discover that you wrote it in an incomprehensible language because you did not convert the keyboard? KeyboSwitcher is a simple tool that helps get your Arabic text without writing it from the beginning. dataset. It achieves 2% top 5 error.
